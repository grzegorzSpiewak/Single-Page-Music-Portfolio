Few information about me

Over last few years I produced and released many songs under many names (Dj Addicted, SideChain Council or Piston G.) – yup, you probably never heard about me. I do have some successes on my list. I created a beat for rap music (you can find it here) won a contest for a remix on nonstop2k.com and finally had on official release on January 2015 on beat-port and itunes.

This is the list of tools and programs which I use:
- Fl Studio, Pro-Tools, Soundforge
- Waves and iZotope plugins
- Nexus, Sylenth1, Massive, Vanguard, Cakewalk Z3TA and tons of other VST

So if I have any question or looking for someone who can make orginal soundtrack to your project contact me.
